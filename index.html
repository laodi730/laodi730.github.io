    "HEALING CAPSULE"

    The Healing Capsule is designed as a home for people who are under stress. The ends of the capsule are spherical spaces that provide physical and emotional healing, and the middle of the capsule is the energy core. The capsule is composed of four ETFE sheetings and two air bags. The outer air bag mainly serves as structural support (it can float on water or land), and the inner air bag forms a responsive inflatable skin that can be inflated to form different “furniture” according to human behavior. Air is one of the most important materials for the Healing Capsule, so the whole construction process is cost-effective, environmentally friendly, energy-efficient and provides a soft and comfortable space for the users.
